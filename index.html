Greg Samuelson

Hi, I'm Greg and I am in the process of becoming a full stack developer thanks to the intensive bootcamp at UCI.

Being in the process is the space I love to find myself in, because I am all about personal development. So far my life has been spent in the pursuit of helping others reach their full potential. From the incredibly rewarding work of starting and running a physical therapy center for kids with cerebral palsy in a poor neighborhood in Southern Morocco to coaching a volleyball team full of guys from Eritrea that had never played the sport before, my biggest thrills have come from seeing someone do what they thought was impossible. I have been an educator my whole life, but more than that, I’ve been a learner.

My ability to take what I’ve learned in one domain and apply it someplace new is one of my greatest strengths and what will make me a great asset to your team. Managing projects, running university campuses, learning languages, improving courses through technology, inventing games, solving problems, keeping budgets under control… these are just a few of the skills I bring with me into the world of coding.

If you are looking for someone with all the front-end and back-end skills to make an impact on day one along with the life experience to make your dynamic team run smoothly, give me a call.
